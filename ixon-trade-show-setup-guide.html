How It Works Day-of-Show
✏️
Make changes — mark booths visited, add notes, log contacts. Changes auto-save to your phone AND sync to the cloud every few seconds.
🔄
Hit the refresh button to force a sync anytime. It pushes your changes up and pulls everyone else's down.
📱
Works offline too. If you lose WiFi on the show floor, your data saves locally. Next time you have signal, hit refresh.
🚫
Closing the browser does NOT lose your data. It's saved on your device. Just re-open the link.
